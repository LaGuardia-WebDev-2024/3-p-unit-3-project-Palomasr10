Recipe #1: Pina Coladas

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Ice 1/4 Cup
Unsweetened Pineapple Juice 3/4 Cup
Unsweetened Coconut Milk 3/4 Cup
Marachino Cherries 1-2

Step 1: Gather contents and put pineapple juice and coconut milk in the blender and blend for about 5-10 seconds until fully blended
Step 2: Add in ice and blend until fully mixed and for additional time depending on desired thickness of the drink
Step 3: Pour into glasses and garnish with a marachino cherry and enjoy!

Source: the Spruce Eats

Recipe #2: Empanadas Under the Sea

  • Time: 2 Hours
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 3 Cups
Butter 1 Stick
Kosher Salt 1/3 Teaspoon
Olive Oil 2 Tablespoons
Shrimp 10-15
Chorizo Sausage 1 Link
Poblano Pepper 2
Corn 1 Ear
Egg 1
Food Coloring 1-2 Drops

Step 1: Sift flour and seperate into 3 seperate bowls with 1 cup of flour in each
Step 2: Melt butter and then add the olive oil and mix well
Step 3: Divide butter and oil mixture evenly, and mix with bowls of flour seperately and using your hands, then add different colors of food coloring to each bowl
Step 4: Refrigerate dough for 30 minutes
Step 5: While the dough refrigerates, begin the filling by cooking the corn, cooking the chorizo, roasting the poblano peppers and cutting the shrimp (keep the ends with the tail)
Step 6: Peel the skin off of the peppers and cut up, then mix all contents together in a bowl
Step 6: Take small parts of each color dough, form into cylinders, line up together and twist; after twisting roll into a ball then using a rolling pin make flat tortillas; repeat until there is only a little of each color dough left
Step 7: Put tails so that they slight stick out of one of the side of the tortilla, then fill and fold over into a semi-circle using a fork to bind the two sides together on the edges; repeat until all tortillas are filled
Step 7: Use leftover dough to create fish scales and eyes and add on top of empanadas
Step 8: Coat in beaten egg on top of empanada
Step 9: Put in the oven at 425 degrees for 20 minutes
Step 10: Let them cool off and enjoy!
